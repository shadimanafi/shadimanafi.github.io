Hi, I am Shadi Manafi.

An AI researcher working on NLP, Vision and their relationship.

I am researching how a machine can learn to understand and interpret the surrounding environment through vision and language. My study focuses on interpreting deep neural networks (DNNs) to extract the knowledge hidden inside them. I have a wealth of experience applying different deep learning models such as CNNs, RNNs, LSTMs, GANs, and Transformers to different tasks in the fields of NLP and computer vision. In addition, I investigate linking visual information with text features.

Project 1: Linking NLP and VISION

This project has been accomplished in two separate phases. First, it investigates the concrete words with the same affordances (e.g., desk and table). Second, because the same concrete words seem to have similar visual features, an unknown class would be labeled using the similar and known class.

Project 2: Analyzing the SCAN paper

This project evaluates the claim of SCAN paper which proposes learning of unseen images. It indicates that SCAN generates new colors by changing only RGB values (numerical, not conceptual), while the generation phase must include new types of object.

  • VAE
  • Python

Project 3: Covid Twitter Misinformation

This approach predicts the effectiveness of a tweet on people reactions to Covid in media by classifying the tweets to three different categories (unpopular, regular, popular tweet) based on the probable number of retweets which a Covid-19 tweet in Twitter specificely can obtain.

  • PyTorch
  • GLOO
  • NLTK
